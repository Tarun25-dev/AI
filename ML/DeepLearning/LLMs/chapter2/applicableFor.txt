What LLMs are GOOD at

➡ Explaining concepts
➡ Writing text
➡ Summarizing content
➡ Generating code
➡ Answering common questions

What LLMs are not GOOD at

➡ Real-time facts
➡ Deep reasoning without guidence
➡ Making decisions alone

Overview of this chapter :
 LLMs generate answers word by word using context and probability, not true understanding.
